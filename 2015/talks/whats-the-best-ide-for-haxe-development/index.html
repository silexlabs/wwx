--- layout: talk year: 2015 title: What's the best IDE for Haxe development? authors: ["Srikanth Ganapavarapu"] slide-url: "" video-url: [] --- In this lightning talk, Srikanth will share an overview of progress made in enhancing Haxe language support in IntelliJ IDEA. It will touch upon high level technical details on work done and the roadmap ahead. The talk shall include a short survey of existing IDEs (including FlashDevelop). This session will help audience appreciate why it makes sense for the Haxe developer community to adapt IntelliJ IDEA as their first choice - be it for enterprise level development needs or as an individual programmer.
