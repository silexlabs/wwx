--- layout: talk year: 2015 title: The tech behind Evoland 2 authors: ["Nicolas Cannasse"] slide-url: "" video-url: [] --- Evoland 2 is an upcoming game by Shiro Games, involving both 2D and 3D graphics, and entirely written in Haxe. Nicolas will introduce the different technologies that were used to develop the game, and which are all available as open source, as well as the workflow the team of 9 people used to build the game.
