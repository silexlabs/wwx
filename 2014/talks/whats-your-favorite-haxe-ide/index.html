--- layout: talk year: 2014 title: What's your favorite Haxe IDE authors: ["Jean-Baptiste Richardet"] slide-url: "ide-slides.pdf" video-url: ["ubrwnb6yzFU"] --- What’s your favorite ide ? That’s a real question in the Haxe community. From the independent developer to the 20 men team, tools can vary. Are you sure you’ve got the one that suits you needs? This presentation will briefly present to you the major tools for Haxe development and let you decide, what’s your favorite ide.
