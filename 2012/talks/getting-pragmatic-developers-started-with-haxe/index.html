--- layout: talk year: 2012 title: Getting pragmatic developers started with Haxe authors: ["Robert Bak"] slide-url: "" video-url: ["Z_da0kJdxaA"] --- We’re all happy early adopters of Haxe and know that the quality of Haxe itself and the libraries is great and getting better. But if an “Average Programmer Joe” finds out he could use something called Haxe for his project, is he likely to pick it up? I’ll talk about the issues people are experiencing while starting to use Haxe and what we can do about them to help with Haxe adoption.
