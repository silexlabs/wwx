--- layout: speaker speaker: Nickolay Grebenshikov title: Head of Lanit Tercom, a software development centre in Abakan --- Nickolay lives in Abakan in Russia. He started working as a programmer in 2000, making web sites using PHP. All his professional career is related to the Internet, especially to the Web. The Haxe apperead in his life in 2008, when he was part of comapping.com team working for the russian outsource company Lanit Tercom. After that he moved to the Microsoft stack and developed the web systems using silverlight and .NET MVC. Now his main responsibility is keeping the Abakan office working and make people happy inside. He also works on several open source projects - https://github.com/ngrebenshikov. Especially Jive, (a cross-platform UI based on AsWing) and openfl-snapsvg (an OpenFL HTML5 backend based on Adobe's Snap.SVG library)
